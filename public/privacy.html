Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 30, 2025

FinBudi ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Email address and Name (optional) for account creation and authentication.
  • Financial Information: Transaction data, budgets, goals, and categories you enter into the App.
  • Device Information: Device model, OS version, and unique device identifiers for analytics and troubleshooting.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use the App, such as feature usage and session duration.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the App.
  • Process your transactions and manage your financial data.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages.
  • Authenticate your identity (via Email or Biometrics).

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for Cloud Messaging (notifications). These providers may access limited data solely to perform services on our behalf.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government request.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new owner.

4. Biometric Data

The App offers an optional Biometric Lock feature (Fingerprint/Face ID). We do not collect or store your biometric data on our servers. Authentication is handled locally on your device using the device's secure hardware.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): To deliver push notifications. Privacy Policy

6. Advertisements

We do not display advertisements in the current version of the App. If ads are introduced in future versions, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect such changes.

7. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Secure Storage: Your financial data is stored securely on our servers.
  • Secure Authentication: We use industry-standard authentication methods.

8. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You can request account deletion directly within the App (Settings > Delete Account). Upon deletion request, your data will be permanently removed from our servers after a 30-day grace period.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Opt-Out: Disable notifications in your device settings.

10. International Users (GDPR/CCPA)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or California, you have rights regarding your data processing, including the right to object to processing and the right to withdraw consent.

11. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
